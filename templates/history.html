About Us

The Heartbeat of the Webmasters

Formed in 1997, the University of Cebu Drum and Bugle Corps is an elite ensemble of student-musicians. We bring rhythm, precision, and immense school pride to campus events, athletic games, and major civic festivals across the region.

Our Legacy & Leadership

Established under the UC Cultural Services division, the corps has spent decades serving as a cultural ambassador for the university.

Guided by Mr. Rudy Aviles, Director of Cultural Affairs, the ensemble operates with a dual mission: achieving competitive artistic excellence on the field while fostering deep personal discipline and leadership off the field.

Under this administrative vision, hundreds of UC students have transformed their musical passion into life-long skills of teamwork, resilience, and academic balance.

Mr. Rudy Aviles
